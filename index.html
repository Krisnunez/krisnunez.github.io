About Me

Hey! Im Kris Nunez, a first year freshman at Wheaton College in Norton, Massachusetts. I'm orginally from New York City, the city of bright lights and loud streets. I live back home with my mom, dad, and two little sisters. Growing up in a immigrant household luckily instilled values of hardwork and discipline into my everyday routine so I really don't like giving up on tasks till I've reached my goal. But I also have my family here on campus away from home or as others call us my "Posse." "Posse?" you're probably asking yourself. In the beginning of my senior year, I was nominated for a oppertunity to be selected to attend a university on a full ride through a foundation called Posse. Posse finds the most outstanding leaders in the classroom and their communities in big cities around the US. Once the ten out of 3000 are selected after a long process of interviews and showcases, Posse supports them through their college careers in montary terms and through mentoring. Not only that but those ten who make it as finalists then become your family for the next 4 years to assure that we stay grounded and loved. I'm truly blessed to be one of ten here at Wheaton. Some of my hobbies include running track here at Wheaton on the Men's Track and Field team, teaching myself stocks, running my clothing brand, videography, and lastly just trying meeting new people. I've always been passionate about being able to create new things I can use to make my everyday life easier, therefore taking Intro to Fabrication was a no-brainer! I don't have much experience yet, but I'm enjoying this new journey of learning how to work with new programs and systems. It's going to be an intresting ride and I hope you stick around to see me grow.
